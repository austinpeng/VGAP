Reg CF Strategy

Using our network and industry expertise, VGAP aims to prepare each partnering platform for a Reg CF equity crowdfunding campaign. Our Reg CF strategy empowers partner to retain maximum control over equity size and executive freedom, while also tapping into their most passionate supporters for a source of self-sufficient funding. In addition, VGAP offers Reg CF consulting services to the public.
